Academics at Highbury

ZIMSEC-based teaching from ECD A‚ÄìB through Grade 6 with a strong focus on reading, writing, numeracy, science, and Christian character. Small classes (1:20) ensure each learner receives the attention they need.

Curriculum

We follow the ZIMSEC curriculum with continuous assessment and termly reporting. English and Mathematics are the core, supported by Science/General Knowledge, ICT, and Languages (English and Shona).

Teaching Approach

Differentiated instruction, guided reading, and practical activities. Weekly checks and targeted intervention ensure steady progress for every learner.

Support & Enrichment

Homework support, literacy circles, and enrichment tasks for high achievers. Character and leadership development are embedded through our Christian ethos.

Mission

To help children reach their full potential by providing a stimulating and happy environment. We nurture curiosity, creativity, and a desire for learning through a balanced curriculum of play activities, interactive learning experiences, and individualized instruction.

Vision

To empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate, and value knowledge and skills as lifelong learners, able to participate in and contribute to the global world while practicing the core values of the school.
